Network map of civil society climate adaptation projects in Vanuatu

This is a network map of climate adaptation projects in Vanuatu in 2016. Click or hover over an organization or activity to see connections. The Vanuatu Climate Adaptation Network (VCAN) is facilitated by Oxfam - if you have knowledge about projects and organizations to add to the map, please share it with priscillas @ oxfampacific . org , then Priscilla will share it with me to update too.
